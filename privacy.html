Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

The short version: Sit With Us does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no ads, and no third-party tracking.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect any personal data from you. Sit With Us does not ask you to create an account, sign in, or provide your name, email address, location, or any other personal information in order to play.

2. On-device data

Your game progress, unlocks, and settings are stored locally on your device so the game remembers where you left off. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us or to anyone else. Deleting the app removes this data.

3. The Daily Puzzle

The shared Daily Puzzle feature connects to the internet to download the same puzzle that all players receive each day. This request does not include any personal information that identifies you.

4. Third parties

Sit With Us does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media tracking. We do not sell or share any data, because we do not collect any.

5. Children's privacy

Sit With Us is suitable for all ages and is designed to be safe for children. Because the app collects no personal information from any user, it collects none from children. We do not knowingly gather data from anyone, regardless of age.

6. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [YOUR-SUPPORT-EMAIL].

About the game

Sit With Us is a cozy high-school seating puzzle. Every lunch, bus ride, and field trip you get just seconds to seat a roomful of students so nobody melts down — match the cliques, pair up the crushes, keep rivals apart, and isolate the troublemakers before the bell rings.

Clear levels to earn points and unlock new rooms, teachers, outfits, and table styles. Take on the shared Daily Puzzle, flip on Chaos mode for double points, and read the Cafeteria Report to see all the drama your seating chart caused.

  • 📅 A new shared Daily Puzzle
  • ⚡ Chaos mode for double points
  • 🎨 Unlockable rooms & table styles

Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Drag each student to a seat before the timer runs out. Keep friends together, rivals apart, and watch the on-screen hints for who gets along with whom. When everyone is happily seated, the level clears.

Does the game cost anything?

Sit With Us is free to download and play.

Do I need an account or internet connection?

No account is required. Most of the game works offline; the shared Daily Puzzle is the only feature that needs a connection.

What is the Daily Puzzle?

It's one special seating challenge that everyone plays each day. Come back daily to keep your streak going.

How do I restore my unlocks?

Your progress is stored on your device. If something looks missing, fully close and reopen the app, and feel free to email us if it persists.
